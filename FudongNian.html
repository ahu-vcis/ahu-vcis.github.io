Biography

I am a PhD student working on computer vision and machine learning in the Visual Computing and Intelligent System Lab (VCIS) at Anhui University, advised by Prof. Teng Li. I work as a visiting student in the National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR) at Institute of Automation Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Prof. Changsheng Xu. I graduated from Anhui University with a BS in College of Electrical Engineering and Automation in 2013 and joined the Communist Party of China in 2016.

My research interests include Computer Vision and Multimedia Content Analysis.
